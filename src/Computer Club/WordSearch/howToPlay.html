How to Play Word Search

Objective

Find all the hidden words in a grid of letters. Words can be hidden in any direction!

Directions Words Can Be Hidden

Horizontal

Vertical

Diagonal

Backwards

How to Play

Look at the word list: You'll see a list of words to find in the puzzle

Scan the grid: Look for the first letter of each word

Check all directions: Once you find the first letter, look in all directions for the rest of the word

Circle or highlight: When you find a word, circle it or highlight it

Cross off the word: Mark the word off your list once found

Keep going: Continue until you've found all the words!

Pro Tips

Start with longer words - they're easier to spot

Look for unusual letters like Q, X, or Z first

Words can share letters with other words

Take breaks if you get stuck - fresh eyes help!
